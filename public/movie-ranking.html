My Best Movies By Emmanuel

My top 4 movies

Fighting Temptation

This movie talks about how we are different in lifestyle, societal strata and circumstances yet we can still enjoy life in our similarities and use our talent, time and substance to show gratitude to God even in our different situations.

I am Gabriel

Iam Gabriel is a movie of hope, trust, faith and dependence in God in our most trying times. we are tempted to throw in the towel most times due to life conditions and challenges at different stages but this movie encourages us to understand that help is on the way... there is always light at the end if the tunnel.

Snow Sword Stride

Snow Sword Stride is a seaonal movie which talks about the ancient Chinese dynasty and some of the conquests of great generals... it talks about family, trust, believe, crime, love, betrayal, dominance, swords and legends. quite interesting

How to Train Your Dragon

How to train your dragon is a cartoon, animated to show how that we can integrate ourselves to interact with our environment, study our surrounding, the terrain, wildlife and use them to our advantage when faced with difficulties and hard times especially from enemies from within.
